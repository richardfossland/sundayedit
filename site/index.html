The genuine innovation №1

You don’t read the whole transcript. You read the amber.

Every word carries a confidence score from the speech model. SundayEdit paints them as colour-coded highlights: the 92% it’s sure about stays clean, the uncertain 8% glows. Press Tab to jump straight to the next word that needs you. Human review gets 10× faster — because you stop proof-reading what was already right.

  • Per-word scores, empirically calibrated against labelled transcripts.
  • Colour-blind safe — every tint is paired with an underline.
  • A live threshold so you decide how careful to be.

The genuine innovation №2

Tell it what the video is about. Watch the names land.

Before transcription, prime SundayEdit with the topic, the speakers, the jargon and the foreign words. Whisper biases recognition toward your glossary — so names and terms come out right the first time, no manual find-and-replace.

Privacy, by architecture

The file never leaves your machine.

Local Whisper runs entirely on your Mac or PC — offline, nothing uploaded, nothing per-minute. Cloud transcription exists for when you want it: off by default, opt-in, with explicit consent. Privacy isn’t a setting here, it’s the default path.

API keys live in the OS keychain. The project file is a local SQLite database. No account required to caption a video.

From import to export

Four steps. Most of them, the AI handles.

  1. 01

    Drop in your video

    MP4, MOV, MKV, WebM — or audio. SundayEdit reads it locally and extracts the waveform.

  2. 02

    Transcribe on your machine

    Pick a Whisper model, add your context & glossary, and let it run. Offline, private.

  3. 03

    Fix only the amber

    Tab through the words the model flagged. Accept alternates, retime on the timeline.

  4. 04

    Style & export

    Design the look, preview WYSIWYG, then export a sidecar or burn it into the video.
